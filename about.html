was as yet thought about an extravagance. Our mission to give a genuinely global affair and mix a really
Italian cuppa, drove us to make the brand ‘COFFEE FACTORY’ . Leading the Indian bistro culture,
‘COFFEE FACTORY’ was a chance to find both the espresso and the art. Nobody has aced espresso superior
to the Italians. Tipping our caps their direction, ‘Organization Name’ conveys new significance to Italian espresso
and eats in the Indian subcontinent. Today, we keep on blending espresso the manner in which world does and in
our adventure we have made espresso associations with our clients crosswise over
India, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Bangladesh, Nepal and Myanmar.
